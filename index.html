Sustainable materials

All EcoCabins have a high insulation value, are produced with sustainable and/or natural materials whenever possible, and have the distinctive visual feature of 'rounded corners,' made possible by the specific use of materials. The advantages of building with wood are numerous: wood is light, flexible, insulates well, and allows for quick and efficient construction.
